# **Cognitive dissonance**.

## It **is**, according to Festinger in 1957,

### a state of **unpleasant tension**,

felt by a person,

### **when** **opposing cognitions** exist in their mind.

## **Experiment** by **Festinger** in **1959**

Subjects are asked to

#### **convince** an accomplice that a **boring task is interesting**.

#### They are given a **reward**, either **1 dollar** or **20 dollars**.

In hindsight,

#### subjects who **lie for little** find the **task more interesting**.
